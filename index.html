About Me

Why Data Science? When I was 8, I spent every Sunday night trying to answer the same question: "Why do the Chicago Bears always lose? Almost twenty years later I still can't answer that question - but at least adversity builds resiliance.. right?

In 2022, I graduated Magna Cum Laude from Loyola University Chicago and was awarded Departmental Honors in Data Science. Despite the lockdown and academic instability, I perservered and spent my time taking on projects in R programming, DS consulting, and visualization across healthcare, and finance, and sports.

I currently work as a Sports Data Quality Assurance Specialist here in Chicago. Along with the operational aspect, I also help develop department foundations, workflow automations, and business reporting. In the last 3 months, I've spearheaded the development of an end-to-end project to automate operational workflow by 60%. See the demo here on Github!

In my free time, I love building custom keyboards, camping & fishing, playing guitar and piano, and thrifting! If you want to own your first custom keyboard, connect with me here!
